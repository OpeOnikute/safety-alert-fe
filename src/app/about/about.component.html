A few weeks ago in Lagos, a tanker explosion occurred.

In the aftermath, people began to realise how much our safety is at risk each day.

Incidents like the tanker falling on commuters in Ojuelegba, and this fella using a cloth to block spilling fuel from a tanker, have made us unsure of our safety.

This small tool we have made, is an attempt to start paying more attention to our safety.

We'll start with collecting and searching for stuff that are a cause for concern about our safety.

Eventually we'll be able to classify locations with the highest safety concerns and receive alerts (push notifications, email, WhatsApp messages etc) about critical safety concerns.

All this won't be possible without your help. So if you have any suggestions, please reach out here.

God bless.
